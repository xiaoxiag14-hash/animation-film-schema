⭐ Sample XML Records

These three sample XML records describe individual animated films and validate against the hosted XSD file.

🎀 Sample Record 1: Your Name.

Open XML
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<animationFilm xmlns="http://example.org/animation-schema"
    xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance"
    xsi:schemaLocation="http://example.org/animation-schema animation.xsd">
    <title>Your Name.</title>
    <director>Makoto Shinkai</director>
    <date>2016</date>
    <genre>Romance</genre>
    <description>A story about body-swapping teenagers who are connected across time and space.</description>
    <animationStyle>Anime</animationStyle>
    <duration>PT106M</duration>
    <inLanguage>Japanese</inLanguage>
    <theme>Time Travel</theme>
    <theme>Identity</theme>
    <productionCompany>CoMix Wave Films</productionCompany>
    <musicDirector>Radwimps</musicDirector>
    <characterDesigner>Masayoshi Tanaka</characterDesigner>
</animationFilm>
