📒 Full Data Dictionary

📌 title

Official title of the animated film. Required, not repeatable, string value.

🎬 director

Person or persons who directed the film. Required, repeatable, string value.

📅 date

Release year of the film. Required, not repeatable, xs:gYear value.

🏷️ genre

Genre category of the animated film. Required, repeatable, controlled vocabulary.

🎨 animationStyle

Visual or production style of animation. Required, repeatable, controlled vocabulary.

💬 theme

Main theme of the film. Optional, repeatable, controlled vocabulary.

🎵 musicDirector

Person or group responsible for the film music or soundtrack. Optional, repeatable.

🧸 characterDesigner

Person responsible for character design. Optional, repeatable.

🔗 isPartOf

Series, franchise, or larger work that the film belongs to. Optional, not repeatable.
